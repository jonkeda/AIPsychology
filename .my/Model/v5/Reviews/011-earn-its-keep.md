# Earn-Its-Keep Audit — Model V5

## TL;DR

- **Cut** S0 Baseline entirely (Baseline). No explanatory power, no operational use, no falsifiability. The model is stronger if it starts at S1 Initial Encounter.
- **Demote** the S3B Bargaining/S7A Maturity heuristic distinction from testable claim to "currently untested." Requires longitudinal observation and self-report.
- **Delete** the Cultural Variability section. Explicit conjecture with zero cross-cultural data gathered.
- **Demote** three other claims (S5 Understanding dual-component, S7F Evangelism S3 Ego Shock-prerequisite, frequency of cycling) marked as internal conjecture but not flagged clearly in main text. Move to "Limits of operationalization."
- **Keep** all S1 Initial Encounter, S2T Trust Evaluation, S2D Defensive Resistance, S3 Ego Shock, S3B Bargaining, S5 Understanding, S6A Enthusiastic Overuse–D, S7A Maturity–F. Each earns its place on explanatory power, operational use, or falsifiability.
- **Keep** both axes, all mechanism anchors, dropout plane, reversibility test, trigger layer, context layer, Limits section.
- Delete 1 state, delete or demote 4 claims, simplify 1 heuristic. V6 is **cleaner** and more intellectually honest.

## Findings

### Keep (16 core constructs)

1. **S1 Initial Encounter.** Novelty appraisal explains the fork (Identity Stakes determines path). Falsifiable.

2. **S2T Trust Evaluation.** Calibration under uncertainty is falsifiable and operationally actionable. Distinguishes rational testing from ego defense.

3. **S2D Defensive Resistance.** Identity-protection cognition explains asymmetric updating. Intervention differs from S2T Trust Evaluation: acknowledge the threat rather than marshaling evidence.

4. **S3 Ego Shock.** Self-efficacy collapse plus terror management is the emotional core. The Stakes × D-Level table provides strong explanatory power.

5. **S6A Enthusiastic Overuse.** Variable-reward reinforcement explains compulsive engagement. Distinct driver from S6B Dependent Overuse/C/D.

6. **S6B Dependent Overuse.** Self-efficacy displacement (confidence in AI replaces confidence in self) is observable. Rebuild independent judgment alongside use.

7. **S6C Driven Overuse.** Goal substitution explains productivity-pressure-driven excess. Requires structural intervention, not just tool feedback.

8. **S6D Social Overuse.** Normative social influence (Asch) is well-established. Explains why the same person adopts at different intensities in different peer groups.

9. **S7A Maturity.** Metacognitive calibration distinguishes healthy scoping from defensive scoping.

10. **S7B Identity Expansion.** Tool incorporation into self-concept (hammer and carpenter; camera and photographer) explains generative adoption.

11. **S7C Ethical Integration.** Meaning-making explains sustained reflection without adoption refusal.

12. **S7D Burnout / Withdrawal.** Resource depletion is the right mechanism. Distinguishes from dropout by trajectory.

13. **S7F Evangelism Influence / Evangelism.** Generativity (Erikson) explains mentoring-driven identity. Predicts who helps others through the cycle.

14. **Both axes.** Stakes predicts fork and intensity; D-level predicts intensity within states. Operationally observable and falsifiable.

15. **Dropout as two-axis plane.** Two independent axes explain identical surface behaviors from different causes. Reversibility test provides clean operationalization.

16. **Mechanism anchors, Limits section, trigger and context layers, loop property, ⚡ convention.** All earn their place.

### Weaken (5 claims)

17. **S3B Bargaining/S7A Maturity heuristic distinction.** The model itself states: "This is a heuristic, not a measured fact. Requires longitudinal observation across multiple model releases, and is contaminated by self-report once a subject has read this model." Explicitly fails falsifiability. Move to "Limits of operationalization."

18. **S5 Understanding dual-component stability claim** (internal reframing plus social normalization). The model states: "Currently conjecture. No instrument is specified for either component." Action: rewrite main text — "Stability appears to involve internal model coherence and ambient social normalization, but neither is operationalized and the claim is untested."

19. **S7F Evangelism S3 Ego Shock-prerequisite claim.** The model states the S3 Ego Shock prerequisite "is currently conjecture." Remove from main text; move to Limits as testable hypothesis ("S3 Ego Shock experience predicts stable S7F Evangelism").

20. **D1–D4 as sharply bounded categories.** The four levels are qualitatively useful but boundaries are intuitive. Where does "summarize article" end and "write report with guidance" begin? Add a note: these are rough qualitative buckets, not precise categories.

21. **"Most people cycle through the model more than once."** The model states "V5 deliberately avoids numerical claims about cycle frequency," yet includes a frequency claim without evidence. Rewrite to "People can cycle through the model as AI evolves; V5 makes no claims about frequency or prevalence."

### Delete (2 constructs)

22. **S0 Baseline.** Fails all three tests. No explanatory power (it's the absence of state). No operational use (practitioners don't assess "at S0 Baseline?"). No falsifiability (tautological by definition). The model is stronger starting at S1 Initial Encounter. Replace with: "Entry point is triggered by meaningful AI exposure (demonstration, mandate, peer adoption, media narrative, personal experience)."

23. **Cultural Variability section.** The model itself states: "This entire section is currently conjecture. No cross-cultural data has been gathered." Speculative on explanatory power, speculative on operational use, untestable as written. Move to Limits with a research proposal, not in main text.

## Recommendations

1. **Delete S0 Baseline entirely.** Rewrite the state diagram to begin at S1 Initial Encounter. Update the entry point: "Entry: Triggered by meaningful exposure to AI (demonstration, workplace mandate, peer adoption event, media narrative, personal experience)."

2. **Reorganize the Limits of operationalization section** to add a subsection — **Untested but Falsifiable Hypotheses** — containing: S3B Bargaining/S7A Maturity heuristic distinction; S5 Understanding dual-component stability claim; S7F Evangelism S3 Ego Shock-prerequisite claim; frequency-of-cycling claim; cultural variability (propose test across five cultural contexts).

3. **Rewrite the S3B Bargaining section.** Remove the S3B Bargaining/S7A Maturity heuristic from main text. Flag it: "See Limits of operationalization for testability status."

4. **Rewrite S5 Understanding.** Downgrade dual-component claim: "Stability appears to involve both an internal mental-model shift and social normalization, but neither is operationalized and the claim is untested."

5. **Rewrite S7F Evangelism.** Remove the S3 Ego Shock-prerequisite claim from main text: "Most observed S7F Evangelism users have passed through S3 Ego Shock (their lived experience makes them credible guides), but this is untested."

6. **Add a note to D1–D4:** "These are qualitatively distinct buckets, not sharply bounded categories. Practitioners should treat them as a rough scale."

7. **Delete the Cultural Variability section** from main model. Move to Limits as: "**Future research: cross-cultural validation.** Preliminary hypothesis: core state structure is culturally invariant; intensity and emotional flavor shift by culture. Proposed test: measure state sequence and dwell time across high craft-identity, techno-optimistic, collectivist, hierarchical, and economic-anxiety cultures. A finding that would falsify the model: the existence of a culture where stable S7A Maturity users regularly enter directly from S0 Baseline without S1 Initial Encounter."
