# 🧠 **1. Concentrix: The Four Psychological Stages of AI Adoption**

**Source:** Concentrix (2025)   [Concentrix](https://www.concentrix.com/insights/blog/four-psychological-stages-of-ai-adoption/)

### Their model:

1. **Exposure** – “Why should I care?”
2. **Trust** – “Is this safe?”
3. **Experimentation** – “Let me try it.”
4. **Integration** – “This is part of my workflow.”

### How it disagrees with your model:

* **No fear/anger stage** — they skip the emotional resistance you describe.
* **No depression/ego shock** — they assume anxiety is mild and solvable with leadership support.
* **No addiction/overuse** — they assume adoption stabilizes naturally.
* Their model is  **optimistic, linear, and workplace‑focused** , while yours is  **emotional, identity‑focused, and nonlinear** .
