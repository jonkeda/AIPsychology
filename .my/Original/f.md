## **TLDR: The Real Psychological Journey of AI Adoption**

Most people don’t adopt AI in a smooth, rational way. They go through a messy emotional cycle:

1. **Ignorance** — “AI won’t work, so I’m not using it.”
2. **Fear/Anger** — “I’ll try it, but I’ll look for reasons to reject it.”
3. **Depression** — “If AI can do all this… what is my worth?”
4. **Understanding** — “It’s just a tool. I can learn to use it.”
5. **Addiction** — “This is amazing. I want to use it for everything.”
6. **(Emerging)** — People eventually land in either:
   * **Maturity** (balanced use),
   * **Identity expansion** , or
   * **Burnout/withdrawal** .

It’s not a tech adoption curve — it’s an identity transformation curve.
